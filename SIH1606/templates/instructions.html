Post-Detection Guidelines

Drone Detected

Immediate action: Alert security personnel.

Guidelines for further investigation: Check if the drone poses any threat based on proximity and speed.

Escalation options: Set a protocol for capturing further data on the drone's path or take necessary actions (e.g., alert authorities).

Bird Detected

No immediate action needed: System will log the detection, and no further steps are required unless unusual flight patterns are observed.
